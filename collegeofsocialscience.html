The College of Arts and Social Sciences, now CoHSS, was established on January 4, 2005, along with the other five colleges at KNUST, following the promulgation of new statutes.
The CoHSS now comprises three faculties: Law, Social Science, and the School of Business, along with a research center.
It initially comprised four academic faculties: Art, Law, School of Business, and Social Sciences, along with the Centre for Cultural and African Studies.
